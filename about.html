My Background

I am currently studying education and preparing for a future career in the classroom. My goal is to become a teacher who creates engaging lessons, supports students academically and emotionally, and encourages curiosity in everyday learning.

My personal experiences and family background have shaped the way I view education. My parents immigrated to the United States and worked extremely hard to build a life for our family. Their determination has influenced my own work ethic and my commitment to helping students believe in their abilities.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe that every student has the ability to learn and succeed when they are given encouragement, structure, and meaningful opportunities to grow. Students thrive when they feel comfortable expressing their ideas and when their classroom environment promotes respect and collaboration.

Creativity and exploration should play an important role in learning. When students are actively involved in the learning process, they are more likely to retain information and develop a deeper understanding of concepts.

Future Goals

As I continue my education and teaching experiences, I hope to build a classroom environment where students feel confident taking risks in their learning. I want to design lessons that are engaging, creative, and meaningful for students with different learning styles.

This website will serve as a portfolio where I can share teaching ideas, classroom resources, and examples of my growth as an educator.
