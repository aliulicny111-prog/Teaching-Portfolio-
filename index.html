Ulicny Creative Classroom is a professional portfolio designed to showcase my growth as a future educator. This site highlights my teaching philosophy, classroom ideas, and the creative strategies I hope to bring into my future classroom.

My Approach to Teaching

I believe that education should inspire curiosity, creativity, and confidence in every student. A classroom should be a space where students feel safe to ask questions, explore new ideas, and develop their own understanding.

Student-centered learning is an important part of my teaching philosophy. Students should actively participate through discussion, creativity, and problem solving.

This website will grow as I gain experience and will include lesson ideas, resources, and reflections.
